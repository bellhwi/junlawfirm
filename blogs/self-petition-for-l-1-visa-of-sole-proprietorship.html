Self Petition for L1 Visa of Sole Proprietorship

October 24, 2023

The Policy Manual update affirms the existing guidance. The update distinguishes a sole proprietor from a self-incorporated petitioner (such as a corporation or a limited liability company with a single owner), where the corporation or the single member limited liability company is a separate and distinct legal entity from its owner, which may petition for that owner.
Please click the link if you would like to read more in the Policy memo.
