About

ATTORNEYS FOR FOREIGN-BORN INDIVIDUALS AND SMALL BUSINESS OWNERS

Hong-min Jun is an attorney specializing in legal affairs for immigrant communities throughout Indiana and Illinois such as Immigration, Personal Injury and Wills & Trusts

As an attorney practicing immigration law, personal injury, and wills and trusts, I am dedicated to being a trusted legal advocate for individuals and families within the immigrant communities of Indiana and Illinois.

With a strong commitment to addressing the personal and unique legal needs of my clients, I specialize in providing comprehensive and compassionate legal services, focusing on immigration matters, personal injury cases, and estate planning. My goal is to help my clients navigate the legal complexities in these areas while ensuring they feel heard, supported, and well-represented in their personal legal affairs.

National Interest Waiver

If you are holding a Master Degree or higher and consider yourself to be contributory to the development of U.S national interest and can prove it, you may petition yourself for Lawful Permanent Resident on your own!

learn more

Marriage Green Card

If you are the spouse of a US citizen, no matter where you were married, you may be eligible to apply for Lawful Permanent Residence (also called “Green Card”) through either Consular processing or Adjustment of Status.

learn more

Treaty Investment Visa (E2)

If you have a dream of becoming an entrepreneur, you make it come true by Treaty Investment visa/status (E2). With E2 visa/status, you can stay in the US doing a business you dreamed to do. The status is valid for two years.
