Featured Services

National Interest Waiver

If you are holding a Master Degree or higher and consider yourself to be contributory to the development of U.S national interest and can prove it, you may petition yourself for Lawful Permanent Resident on your own!

learn more

Green Card Through Marriage

If you are the spouse of a US citizen, no matter where you were married, you may be eligible to apply for Lawful Permanent Residence (also called “Green Card”) through either Consular processing or Adjustment of Status.

Hong-min Jun, Attorney

Law Office of Hong-min Jun, is a law firm dedicated to providing strong legal representation for clients throughout the state of Indiana and Illinois. Our firm serves a diverse client base with matters regarding immigration matters, personal injury cases, and estate planning. Law Office of Hong-min Jun, P.C distinguishes itself by helping clients navigate the legal complexities in these areas while ensuring they feel heard, supported, and well-represented in their personal legal affairs. learn more

Attorney Jun has assisted a diverse range of immigrant and foreign national groups with various objectives, including investors, students, families, employers, companies, churches, and employees. Notably, he has volunteered his time, knowledge, and experience as an attorney for numerous foreign student groups across the Midwest, including Korean Student Associations at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), Ohio State University (OSU), University of Wisconsin, Cincinnati University, Indiana University at Bloomington (IUB), Purdue University, and the University of Kentucky (UK).
He has also served as a director of Relations for the Korean Scientists and Engineers of Indiana Chapter (KSEA-Indiana) and as a mentor for K Movement of the Korean Trading Agency in New York (KOTRA). Attorney Jun has conducted various seminars at different venues upon request, covering topics such as changes in immigration laws and regulations, explanations of immigration options, including H1b, OPT, National Interest Waiver (NIW), Marriage Green Cards, E2, and more.
