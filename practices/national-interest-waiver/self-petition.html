Are you an ambitious professional in the fields of medicine, engineering, academia, or technology, seeking to unlock your full potential in the United States? Look no further! The Law Office of Hong-Min Jun P.C. is here to serve you, catering to individuals like you who are ready to take their careers to new heights through the National Interest Waiver (NIW) program.
Over the years, the Law Office of Hong-min Jun P.C. has achieved remarkable success in securing NIW approvals for clients across various trades and industries. Our expertise extends to a wide range of fields, including but not limited to:
  • Medicine and Healthcare: We have successfully represented doctors, surgeons, medical researchers, and other healthcare professionals who have made significant contributions to medical advancements, patient care, and public health.
  • Engineering and Technology:Our clients from the engineering and technology sectors have been pioneers in their fields, driving innovation, developing groundbreaking technologies, and enhancing the overall infrastructure.
  • Academia and Research: We have assisted professors, researchers, and scholars who have demonstrated exceptional research skills, published influential papers, and made substantial contributions to their respective fields of study.
  • Science and Innovation: Our track record includes clients in scientific research, bioinformatics, biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and other scientific domains, whose work has led to breakthrough discoveries and advancements.
  • Business and Entrepreneurship: We have guided entrepreneurs, business executives, and startup founders who have demonstrated exceptional business acumen, job creation potential, and economic growth prospects.
At the Law Office of Hong-min Jun P.C., our in-depth understanding of the specific requirements and nuances of each trade allows us to craft compelling NIW petitions tailored to showcase your unique qualifications. We meticulously analyze your professional achievements, accolades, research contributions, publications, patents, and more to build a robust case that showcases your exceptional abilities.
Our proven track record speaks for itself, with numerous clients who have obtained NIW approvals and gone on to achieve remarkable success in their chosen fields. Their achievements range from groundbreaking medical research and technological advancements to influential academic contributions and entrepreneurial endeavors.
